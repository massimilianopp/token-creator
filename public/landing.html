How it works

Four steps.
One complete launch.

Every tool you need to go from idea to live token with liquidity — in a single app.

1
🪙 Create your token

Define your token name, symbol, description, and upload your logo. Your image is pinned to IPFS via Pinata for permanent decentralized storage. The token is created on-chain as an SPL token with Metaplex metadata. Choose your supply, decimals, and dev allocation — then optionally revoke mint and freeze authorities for a fully trustless token.

SPL Token Metaplex IPFS / Pinata
2
🔒 Set up vesting

Lock your dev allocation using Streamflow, a fully audited vesting protocol on Solana. Configure a cliff period, total vesting duration, and recipient wallet. Tokens unlock linearly and transparently on-chain — no trust required. Create the vesting contract before revoking authorities to keep the process fully compatible.

Streamflow On-chain vesting
3
💧 Add liquidity

Create a concentrated liquidity pool on Orca Whirlpool — Solana's leading AMM. Choose your pair (token / SOL or token / USDC), set an initial price, and deposit. The app handles pool initialization, tick array setup, SOL wrapping, and position opening automatically. Your position is a Whirlpool NFT you fully own.

Orca Whirlpool Full-range position WSOL auto-wrap
4
📊 Share your public page

Every token gets a shareable public page at /token/[mint]. It displays live price data from DEXscreener, an embedded price chart, top holders, circulating supply, vesting contract status, and your social links — all pulled from on-chain metadata automatically.

DEXscreener Live price Vesting status
